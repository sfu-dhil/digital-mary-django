The
Digital Mary
Project

The Digital Mary Project is a Mariological resource dedicated to the collection and curation of the material culture related to Virgin Mary. Our primary objective is to build a comprehensive open-access database that brings all of the artistic productions, artefacts, and built landscapes related to the early Cult of the Virgin in the Eastern Mediterranean together into an easily-searchable digital platform.
